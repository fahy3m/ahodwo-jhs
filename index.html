🎓 Year-End & Promotion
How this works:
1. Make sure all scores and attendance are entered and correct.
2. Set each student's promotion status in the Attendance page.
3. Click "Archive This Term" to save a permanent copy of all reports for this term.
4. At the start of a new academic year, click "Start New Academic Year" — promoted students move to the next class automatically, repeated students stay.
📦 Step 1 — Archive Current Term

This saves a permanent snapshot of all students, scores and reports for —. You can view archived reports anytime under Past Term Reports.

🎓 Step 2 — Start New Academic Year

This will clear all scores and attendance for the new year. Students marked Promoted will move to the next class. Students marked Repeated stay in this class.
